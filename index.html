Catickory

A local party game where players have to associate a word for a chosen category before time runs out. However, the catch is you can only give an answer starting with a letter no one else has used yet. It's a filler game with a lot of replayability, perfect for boring family gatherings or while waiting for that one friend who's always late. All you need is this device to start playing.

Daydle

You have six tries to guess the year this event happened on this day in history. Every failed attempt gives you a hint that will help you pinpoint the year, meaning you don't have to be a history connoisseur to have fun. After you play a game, you can challenge your friends by sharing your results. Every day is a new Daydle! Inspired by Wordle.
