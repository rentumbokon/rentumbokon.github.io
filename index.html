Daydle

You have six tries to guess the year this event happened on this day in history. Every failed attempt gives you a hint that will help you pinpoint the year, meaning you don't have to be a history connoisseur to have fun. After you play a game, you can challenge your friends by sharing your results. Every day is a new Daydle! Inspired by Wordle

Catickory

A local party game where players have to associate a word for a chosen category before time runs out. However, the catch is you can only give an answer starting with a letter no one else has used yet. It is a quick game with a lot of replayability, perfect for boring family gatherings or when you're waiting dinner to be served. All you need is friends and this one device to start playing.
