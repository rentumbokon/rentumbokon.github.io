<strong>A fun game where you guess the year this event happened on this day in history.</strong>

<h3>Motivation</h3>
A combination of Timelines (board game) and Wordle (web game). 

<h3>Cool Facts</h3>
Part of the Wordle spin-off craze.

<h3>Technologies</h3>
<ul>
    <li>HTML</li>
    <li>CSS</li>
    <li>React JS</li>    
    <li>Spring Boot</li>
